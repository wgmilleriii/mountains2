How to Use

  1. Click on the map to set your standing point (marked with a black square)
  2. Click again to set your viewing point (creates a circle and viewing arc)
  3. The elevation profile between these points will be displayed below
  4. Hover over points in the profile to see exact elevations
  5. Click anywhere on the map again to reset and start over
